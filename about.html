About Me


I, Elaina Miel Reyes, am on track to graduate from the University of Central Florida in Spring 2025 with her B.A. degree in Digital Media and declared special focus in Website Design. Through my time in the program, I have become familiar with the basics in the field such as HTML, CSS, Javascript, PHP, as well as libraries like React and frameworks like React Native.

Since early childhood, I have gravitated towards the arts and nourished my creative interests. After graduating highschool in 2021 from Jacksonville, FL's Atlantic Coast High School, I discovered web design as a medium for my artistic persuits during my freshman year at UCF. I realized that this industry niche provided me with a career-oriented way to let my natural skills for aesthetics and composition truly shine.

Outside of school, I have 4 years of experience in restaurant service industry where I acquired crucial communication skills and sales techniques. As the top server with the highest sales at my current job, I have mastered the arts of customer service and pursuasive selling.
